Being explorative and curious about new technologies is always my kind of thing. Finding a solution to varying problems, I always love that. Ever since childhood, I have always wanted to solve problems. I still remember the first computer programming language I learned in my school was C++ and I stood 1st in a programming competition.

This passion carried me through my Computer Science education at University College London (UCL). Here I found more resources available to me than I ever could have imagined. I studied new languages, algorithms, and higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a kid. I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Computer Programming is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.
