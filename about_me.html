More About Me

Being explorative and curious about new technologies is always my kind of thing. Finding a solution to varying problems, I always loved that. Ever since childhood, I have always wanted to solve problems. I still remember the first computer language I learned in my school was C++ and stood 1st in a programming competition.
This passion carried me through my Computer Science education at University College London (UCL). Here I found more resources available to me than I ever could have imagined. I studied new languages, algorithms, higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a kid. I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Computer Programming is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.
I gave interviews to some of the top FAANG companies including Google, Amazon, and Facebook. After completing my internship with Google I joined Facebook as a software engineer on March 20, 2015. Working in Google was pretty chill though, but over the time I developed a passion about social networking kind of things that's why I have chosen Facebook over the Google.

Thank you!
